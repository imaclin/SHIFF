Why this order?

Stats — after The Show

The reader just learned what the show is about. Hit them with the numbers immediately — before they meet any characters. The injustice lands harder when it's abstract data, not a person's face yet.

Timeline — after The Era

The Era sets the cultural backdrop. The Timeline shows the specific events. Together they give the reader: world → story arc → then characters. Proper narrative build.

Betrayal — between Characters and Players

The reader has just met Kevin and Dale. Now they learn what happened between them. This is the emotional peak of the character section — a gut punch right before the players section shifts the lens to the celebrities.
